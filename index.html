Women Safety Device

Summary
The Women Safety Device is a compact and discreet wearable designed to enhance personal security. Equipped with GPS tracking, emergency alerts, and real-time location sharing, it provides immediate assistance at the press of a button. This device aims to empower women with a reliable tool for their safety, offering peace of mind and swift response in critical situations.

Objective
The device should be subtle and easy to carry or wear, ensuring it does not draw unwanted attention. Enable real-time tracking to provide accurate location data during emergencies. Ensure the device can quickly send alerts to pre-set emergency contacts and authorities.
